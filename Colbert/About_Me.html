STEVE'S LIFE

2026

I joined the UHH faculty in August 2010. My family and I had a big move from Nice, France, where I was studying interactions between bacteria and particles in the lagoon of New Caledonia at the Laboratoire d'Oceanographie de Villefranche and teaching Oceanography at SKEMA Business School. My research interest is focused on nearshore hydrology and the cycling of nutrients in the coastal ocean. When I'm not working, I am either enjoying a game with the family or preparing my next meal at the grocery store or farmer's market!
